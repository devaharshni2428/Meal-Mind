MealMind is an AI-Based Food Waste Prediction and Redistribution System designed to reduce food wastage in institutions such as colleges, hostels, and canteens. The system predicts food requirements based on student attendance and estimates surplus food that can be redistributed through NGOs. The application provides modules for attendance management, food prediction, analytics, reporting, and NGO coordination. By analyzing attendance data, MealMind helps institutions prepare the right amount of food, reducing unnecessary waste and improving resource utilization. The project is developed using Python, Flask, HTML, and CSS. Future enhancements include MySQL database integration, machine learning-based prediction, automated NGO notifications, and mobile application support. MealMind aims to contribute to sustainable food management and support communities by ensuring that excess food reaches people in need.
